**Review lessons (Lesson 5 of every unit)** stay tier-matched to their unit ‚Äî
Unit 4's review is still foundation-level, Unit 8's review is
combination-level, Unit 12's review is bridge-level ‚Äî so pacing doesn't
flatten out at exactly the moments meant to consolidate each tier.

**Self-containment preserved:** every lesson still opens with warm-up +
model text before recycling into practice, so a student starting at
Unit 9 isn't lost ‚Äî the harder load comes from combined grammar,
longer text, and less scaffolding within that unit, not from assuming
knowledge of Units 1‚Äì8's specific vocabulary.
