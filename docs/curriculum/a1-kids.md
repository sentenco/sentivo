# A1 Kids Curriculum

All 12 units of the A1 Kids track, each broken down lesson by lesson.
Every lesson is 25 minutes, built for online 1:1 teaching, with the same
6-stage shape: **Warm-up (3 min) → Presentation (5 min) → Guided practice
(6–7 min) → Freer practice (4–6 min) → Review (2 min) → Wrap-up (2 min)**.

Units 1–2 are already built and live in the app (Supabase). Units 3–12
are fully scripted here, ready to be turned into real Sentivo lessons
when development resumes.

# Unit 3: My Family

## Lesson 1: Family Vocabulary

| Part | Plan |
|------|------|
| Time | 25 minutes |
| Main aim | Name immediate family members (mom, dad, sister, brother, baby, grandma, grandpa) |
| Materials | Family picture slides, flashcards, matching game, quiz slide |

| Stage | Time | Online material / activity | Teacher role |
|-------|------|----------------------------|--------------|
| Warm-up | 3 min | Show a family photo; ask "Who is this?" | Elicit any known words |
| Presentation | 5 min | Family picture reveal: mom, dad, sister, brother, baby, grandma, grandpa | Say each word, model pronunciation, have students repeat |
| Guided practice | 7 min | Repeat and point — point to the correct family member when named | Check answers, praise, correct gently |
| Freer practice | 6 min | Matching game — family words to pictures | Prompt pair or whole-class answers |
| Review | 2 min | Quick quiz: show a picture, students say the word | Confirm understanding, praise |
| Wrap-up | 2 min | Each student names one family member from their own family | Close the lesson warmly |

## Lesson 2: This Is My...

|------|------|

| Warm-up | 3 min | Quick review of family words from Lesson 1 | Fast call-and-response |

| Wrap-up | 2 min | Each student says one "This is my ___" sentence about their own family | Close the lesson warmly |
